Stock API

Welcome to this page

Usage Info: This API provides data on stocks list blog and canadian stock market best perfomrming stocks market data from tmx as source.

Endpoints

  • /stock-list: Fetches stock-list data.
    • Rate Limit: 100 requests per hour
    • Pricing Plan: Free
    • Buy or Upgrade to Paid Plan
  • /topdividend: Fetches Stocks with the highest dividend yield data.
    • Rate Limit: 100 requests per hour
    • Pricing Plan: Free
    • Buy or Upgrade to Paid Plan

Note: Please adhere to the rate limits to ensure smooth operation of the API.

Try going to any of the paths mentioned above to retrieve data.
